## Background

I studied Computer Systems Engineering at [ITESM](https://tec.mx) with a brief but awesome stint at [EPITECH](http://www.epitech.eu) in France.

After college I went to the [Recurse Center](https://recurse.com) and had the time of my life building projects like a Dropbox clone and my own ad-blocker.

I moved to the Bay Area in 2015 to join Wootric as their 2nd hire. I worked there until the company was acquired in 2020. I joined Segment in 2021.
